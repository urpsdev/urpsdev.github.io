About Us

Welcome to Urban And Rural Protection Services, a pioneering force in security services headquartered in Lucknow. As a trailblazer in the realm of safety and protection, we have firmly established our presence across Uttar Pradesh, India. Recognized and authorized under the aegis of the Private Security Agencies (Regulation) Act, 2005, our commitment to excellence is unwavering.

We specialize in a spectrum of security provisions that encompass Security Guard Services, Armed Guard Security Services, Trained Security Guard Services, and even Gunman Security Services. Our expertise extends to VIP and Executive Security Services, Corporate Security Services, and the vigilant protection of Industrial and Domestic domains. We are the vanguard of Project and Event Security Services, and our proficiency encompasses the roles of Personal Bodyguards.

In our pursuit of mastery, we offer comprehensive Security Guards Training and deliver peerless Security Guards Recruitment Services. Our team embodies Security Supervisors of the highest caliber, and our guarding services stand as a paragon of vigilance. Embracing diversity, we proudly extend our services through Lady Guarding Services, Manpower Services, and the dynamic realm of Online E-commerce.
Our ethos revolves around an unswerving pursuit of excellence. We consistently push the boundaries to provide services that not only meet but exceed expectations. Driven by innovation and backed by experience, we shape security solutions that resonate with our clients' distinctive needs. With a steadfast focus on quality and an unwavering pledge to timeliness, Urban And Rural Protection Services is your embodiment of security in an evolving world.
