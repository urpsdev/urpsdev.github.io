About URPS

Urban And Rural Protection Services (URPS) is a Lucknow‑based security and manpower partner serving businesses across Uttar Pradesh. We’ve been operating since 2017 and were incorporated as a private limited company in 2020.

We deploy trained armed/unarmed guards for corporate, residential, hospital, industrial and event sites—backed by 24x7 supervision, fast replacement, and clear reporting. URPS operates with PSARA licensing and is compliant with MSME (Udyam), GST, ESIC and EPFO requirements.
